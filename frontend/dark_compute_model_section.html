Breaking down the H100-years performed before detection

The amount of computation a covert PRC AI project can perform before it's detected depends on three factors: (1) how much unreported AI hardware the PRC can stock (2) how much of this hardware the PRC can operate in covert data centers, and (3) the strength of evidence US intelligence gathers for the project's existence over time. The following break down these factors.
—
Dark Compute Stock
The total stock of undeclared PRC compute is equal to the compute that the PRC initially stashes away, plus whatever compute it can obtain during the agreement. Finally, we need to factor in that some of this compute may start to burn out over time.
Dark Compute = (Initial Dark Compute + Covert Fab Flow) × (Average Survival Rate of Compute)
