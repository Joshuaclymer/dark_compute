Dark Compute Stock Breakdown
The total stock of undeclared PRC compute is equal to the compute that the PRC initially stashes away, plus whatever compute it can obtain during the agreement. Finally, we need to factor in that some of this compute may start to burn out over time.
Dark Compute = (Initial Dark Compute + Covert Fab Flow) × (Average Survival Rate of Compute)
