AI Chip Lifespan

Public data on AI chip lifespan is scarce. We used three sources to estimate hardware survival rates.

Meta's Llama 3 Training
Training on 16,000 H100s experienced 419 unexpected interruptions over 21 million GPU-hours, but only 3 required manual intervention.

Blue Waters (2013–2021)
Approximately 97% of the 4,000 K20X GPUs survived 8 years under high utilization.

Titan (2012–2018)
Between 60–90% of well-cooled GPUs survived 5 years, but only 10–40% of poorly-cooled GPUs did.
