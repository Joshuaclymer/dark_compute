AI Chip Lifespan

How long do AI chips actually last? There's surprisingly little public data on this. We pieced together estimates from three sources:

Meta's Llama 3 Training
Meta trained Llama 3 on 16,000 H100s. Over 21 million GPU-hours, they saw 419 unexpected interruptions—but only 3 required someone to physically intervene. Most failures were software, not hardware.

Blue Waters (2013–2021)
This supercomputer ran 4,000 K20X GPUs for 8 years under heavy use. About 97% of them survived the whole period.

Titan (2012–2018)
Cooling matters a lot. Among well-cooled GPUs, 60–90% survived 5 years. Poorly-cooled ones? Only 10–40% made it.
