Architecture Efficiency

Chip architectures improve over time, delivering more performance independent of transistor density gains. We estimate this by comparing total performance improvements against transistor density improvements.

According to Epoch's data, total performance (TPP/die area) has improved at ~1.55x per year:



However, transistor density has only increased at ~1.26x per year:



Therefore, architecture improvements account for roughly 1.55 / 1.26 = 1.23x per year.
