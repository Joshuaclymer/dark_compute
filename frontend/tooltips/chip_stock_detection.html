Chip Stock Detection

Method:
US intelligence could detect a covert project by noticing missing AI chips from China's reported compute stock.

Key Factors:
1. Amount diverted: Diverting 50% would be obvious; 5% might not be.
2. USG error bars: If estimates show 800K–1.2M H100e, a few percent missing wouldn't raise suspicion. Precise counts would reveal discrepancies.

Historical Calibration:
Intelligence estimates of weapons counts (1960–2025) show median errors of ~15%.



Adjustment:
AI chips flow through commercial supply chains with export controls providing some visibility. Estimated US intelligence error margin: ~7%.
