Energy Efficiency Relative to H100

This multiplier captures how energy-efficient China's chips are compared to an H100. A value of 1.0 means they're equally efficient. Lower values mean China's chips use more power for the same compute:

• 0.5 means Chinese chips use twice as much energy per unit of compute
• 1.0 means they match H100 efficiency
• 2.0 means they're twice as efficient as H100s

We use this to convert H100-equivalent compute into actual power consumption.
