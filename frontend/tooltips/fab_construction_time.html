Fab Construction Time Parameters

Construction time depends on production capacity. For declared fabs, the time from groundbreaking to production ranges from 1 to 3 years.



Secrecy Tax:
Covert fabs likely take longer to build. When comparing declared versus undeclared nuclear enrichment facilities, declared facilities averaged 3.7 years while undeclared facilities averaged 5.5 years, representing a 1.5x "secrecy tax."

A realistic covert fab timeframe is 1.5 to 4.5 years.



Construction Labor:
McKinsey estimates approximately 100 workers per $1B of fab construction cost. The model uses this heuristic: if construction labor falls short, construction slows proportionally, while excess labor provides no speedup.
