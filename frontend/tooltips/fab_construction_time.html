Fab Construction Time

Construction time depends on production capacity. For declared fabs, time from groundbreaking to production ranges from 1–3 years.



Secrecy Tax:
Covert fabs likely take longer. Comparing declared vs. undeclared nuclear enrichment facilities: declared averaged 3.7 years, undeclared averaged 5.5 years—a 1.5x "secrecy tax."

Realistic covert fab timeframe: 1.5–4.5 years.



Construction Labor:
McKinsey estimates ~100 workers per $1B of fab construction cost. The model uses this heuristic: if construction labor falls short, construction slows proportionally. Excess labor provides no speedup.
