PRC SME Indigenization Parameters

Why It Matters:
The global SME market has very few buyers and sellers. Missing equipment would make the PRC an obvious suspect, so a covert fab would need primarily domestic equipment to avoid detection through procurement accounting.

These parameters represent the probability of achieving greater than 90% domestic SME sourcing at each process node by the specified year.



Current Status (2025):
The PRC can manufacture 130nm chips with approximately 80% domestic tools. Advanced nodes require imported equipment, especially photolithography scanners.

Projections (by 2031):
• 28nm: There is approximately a 25% chance of greater than 90% localization, as SMEE and Huawei are developing DUV immersion scanners.
• 14nm: There is approximately a 10% chance, since this node requires multi-patterning with 28nm scanners.
• 7nm: There is approximately a 6% chance of achieving this level of localization.

Key Factors:
• U.S. export controls are driving urgency.
• The government has allocated a $47.5B SME fund.
• Aggressive talent recruitment is underway, with salaries up to 3x those offered by ASML.
