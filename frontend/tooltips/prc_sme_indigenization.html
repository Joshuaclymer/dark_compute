PRC SME Indigenization

Why It Matters:
The global SME market has very few buyers and sellers. Missing equipment would make the PRC an obvious suspect. A covert fab would need primarily domestic equipment to avoid detection through procurement accounting.

These parameters represent the probability of >90% domestic SME sourcing at each process node by the specified year.



Current Status (2025):
The PRC can manufacture 130nm chips with ~80% domestic tools. Advanced nodes require imported equipment, especially photolithography scanners.

Projections (by 2031):
• 28nm: ~25% chance of >90% localization (SMEE and Huawei developing DUV immersion scanners)
• 14nm: ~10% chance (requires multi-patterning with 28nm scanners)
• 7nm: ~6% chance

Key Factors:
• U.S. export controls driving urgency
• $47.5B government SME fund
• Aggressive talent recruitment (3x ASML salaries)

The main bottleneck is talent, not funding.
