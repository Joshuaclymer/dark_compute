Scanner Production Capacity Parameters

Photolithography scanners are often the main bottleneck to chip production.

ASML's DUV machines can pattern ~250 wafers/hour (180K wafers/month). However, each chip requires ~80 patterning steps and scanners operate at ~50% utilization:

0.5 × 180,000 / 80 = ~1,000 completed wafers/month per scanner

Production Function:
Output is the minimum of labor and scanner constraints:

Production = min(24.6 × workers, 1000 × scanners)
