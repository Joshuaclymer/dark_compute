6. Breaking down P(Misalignment after Handoff): After handoff (when AI R&D speedup reaches --x), alignment research is conducted autonomously by AI. Assuming the systems developers handoff to are safe to defer to, misalignment risk after handoff depends on the "alignment tax" - the ratio of alignment research effort to capability research effort. We assume a --x higher alignment tax after handoff is required to achieve the same safety level as during handoff.
The alignment tax paid during handoff is the ratio of adjusted alignment research time to adjusted capability research time during the handoff window.
