Model Parameters

Simulation Settings

Number of takeoff model simulations (with randomly sampled parameters) used to compute uncertainty bands.

P(Misalignment at Handoff)

Probability of AI takeover given the safety-research-speed-adjusted handoff duration (time between AC and SAR).

How much less relevant pre-handoff research is compared to post-handoff research (0 = irrelevant, 1 = equally relevant).

Multiplier for alignment research effort during slowdown (e.g., 1.5 = 50% more effort).

Proportion of compute spent on alignment after handoff (0 to 1). E.g., 0.1 = 10% of compute.

Safety speedup = capability_speedup × multiplier. E.g., 0.5 means safety speedup is half of capability speedup.

Maximum alignment speedup before handoff. Caps the formula: min(max, capability_speedup × multiplier).

P(Misalignment after Handoff)

Probability of misalignment after handoff given the alignment tax (proportion of compute on alignment).

P(Human Power Grabs)

Probability of human power grabs given the raw handoff duration (not adjusted for research speed).

Software Proliferation

Model scenarios where the PRC covert project gains access to leading AI lab software (weights and/or algorithms).

When enabled, the covert project gains the AI capabilities (automation fraction, research taste) of the leading project at the specified milestone.

When enabled, the covert project has the same software efficiency as the leading project up to the specified milestone.
