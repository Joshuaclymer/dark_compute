AI R&D Speedup Trajectory

How This Model Works

This model uses the AI Takeoff Trajectory Predictor to estimate when key AI capability milestones will be reached:

  • AC (Automated Coder): AI can automate software engineering tasks
  • ASI (Artificial Superintelligence): AI far beyond human capability

The model projects global AI R&D compute growing exponentially at 2.25x per year from a 2026 baseline, with a fraction of global compute dedicated to the single largest AI project.

The trajectory shows how quickly capabilities advance when compute resources grow without constraint.
